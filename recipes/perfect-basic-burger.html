Perfect Basic Burger

Description

This is your plain basic burger. It don't get much simpler than this. This will taste better than Five Guys no question. I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  • .5 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • .5 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  • Preheat grill for high heat
  • Whisk together egg, salt, and pepper in medium bowl
  • Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix with hands until well blended.
  • Form four .75 inch patties
  • Place patties on grill and cook 6-8 minutes on each side
  • Serve on a toasted bun
